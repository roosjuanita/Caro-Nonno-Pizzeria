QUATTRO FORMAGGI

Ingredients:
- Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
- 3/4 cup tomato sauce (optional for red base, omit for white pizza)
- 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup shredded provolone cheese
- 1/2 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
- Olive oil for drizzling
- Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll or stretch the dough into a 10–12 inch circle and place on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
3. If using tomato sauce, spread evenly over the dough, leaving a 1 inch crust border.
4. Evenly sprinkle all four cheeses across the pizza surface.
5. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbling.
6. Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with fresh basil before slicing and serving.
