PEPPERONI PIZZA

Ingredients:
- Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- Pizza sauce
- Shredded mozzarella cheese (~12 oz)
- Pepperoni slices (18–20 slices)
- Optional: Ground black pepper and oregano

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C).
2. Roll out the dough and spread an even layer of pizza sauce, leaving a 3/4–1 inch border for the crust.
3. Sprinkle evenly with mozzarella, then arrange pepperoni slices on top.
4. Optionally, finish with a sprinkle of black pepper or oregano.
5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
